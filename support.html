How do I start drawing with a friend?
1. Tap "Create Room" in the app
2. Choose Simultaneous or Turn-Based mode
3. Share the 4-digit room code with your friend
4. Your friend taps "Join Room" and enters the code
5. Start drawing together!

What's the difference between Simultaneous and Turn-Based modes?
Simultaneous: Everyone draws at the same time. Perfect for collaborative art!
Turn-Based: One person draws at a time. Great for games like Pictionary or taking turns on a drawing.
How do I save my drawing?
Tap the "Save" or "Export" button (usually in the top toolbar). The drawing will be saved to your Photos app.
Can I use the Echo feature?
Yes! Tap the "Echo" button to enable it. Use the +/- buttons to adjust the number of echoes (0-10). Your strokes will multiply for cool visual effects!
How long do rooms stay active?
Rooms and drawings are automatically deleted after 24 hours for privacy. Make sure to save any artwork you want to keep!
Do I need to create an account?
No! Draw With Friends requires no login, no account, and no personal information. Just open the app and start drawing.
What iOS version do I need?
Draw With Friends requires iOS 16.0 or later.
Is my data private?
Yes! We don't track you, collect personal information, or store drawings permanently. See our Privacy Policy for details.
