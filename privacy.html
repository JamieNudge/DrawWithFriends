Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 16, 2025

Overview

DrawWithFriends is a collaborative drawing app that values your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how it's used.

What We Collect

Drawing Session Data (Temporary):

  • Session codes and room identifiers
  • Drawing strokes sent between devices
  • Session participation data

Photo Backgrounds (Optional & Temporary):

Important: Photo sharing is completely optional. You choose if and when to import photos.
  • User-Selected Photos: Users can optionally import photos from their device to use as drawing backgrounds
  • Photo Sharing: When you import a photo, it is:
    • Compressed to reduce file size (60% JPEG quality)
    • Uploaded to our servers (Firebase)
    • Shared with all participants in your drawing room
    • Automatically deleted after 24 hours of inactivity (same as drawing data)
  • Consent Required: The app shows a warning before importing photos to ensure you understand they will be shared with other users in your room
  • Your Choice: Only import photos you're comfortable sharing with your drawing partners
  • No Full Library Access: We only access specific photos you choose to import - not your entire photo library

What We DON'T Collect:

  • No names, emails, or phone numbers
  • No user accounts or logins
  • No location tracking
  • No advertising data
  • No personal information
  • No access to your entire photo library - you choose specific photos

How We Use Data

  • Session data powers real-time collaboration between devices
  • Photo backgrounds (if imported) are shared with room participants for collaborative drawing
  • All session data (including photos) is automatically deleted after 24 hours of inactivity
  • We do not sell, share, or use your data for any other purpose
  • Photos are only shared with participants in your specific drawing room - never publicly or with third parties

Data Storage & Security

  • Drawing data is temporarily stored on Firebase (Google Cloud)
  • Imported photos are compressed, encrypted during transmission, and stored temporarily on Firebase
  • All data transmission is encrypted
  • Sessions are automatically deleted after 24 hours of inactivity
  • We do not retain any photos or drawings after sessions end

Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (by Google) to enable real-time drawing collaboration and photo sharing. Firebase may collect technical data such as device type and crash reports to maintain service quality. Firebase Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect personal information. However, we recommend parental supervision when children under 13 use the photo import feature to ensure they only share appropriate images.

Your Rights

  • You can stop using the app at any time
  • All session data (including photos) is automatically deleted within 24 hours
  • You control which photos (if any) you choose to import and share
  • You can clear the canvas and background image at any time during your session

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent changes. Continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
