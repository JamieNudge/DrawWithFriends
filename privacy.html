Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-14

Overview

DrawWithFriends is a small, lightweight collaborative drawing app. This policy explains what data may be collected and how it's used.

What we collect

  • Room identifiers and ephemeral session data needed to run collaborative sessions.
  • Optional contact information if you submit it via support or feedback forms.

How we use data

Session data is used only to power real-time collaboration. We don't sell personal data. Persistent storage is opt-in and will be described where offered.
